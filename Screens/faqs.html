FAQs

What service our site offers?

Our site will issue you a list of equipment that the passenger must take to the destination according to personal data And the passenger's preferences as well as the activities he is going to do.

So, how does it work?

The algorithm calculates a list of items that the passenger must take to the destination according to the passenger's personal data and preferences, activities he is going to do, and also according to the weather at that place.

What should I do to get the list?

All you have to do is click on the option of "New Trip" and enter the details of the trip, do not worry it is short and convenient. And in a moment you have an accurate list that is just right for you.

And what if there are personal things I want to add to or delete from the list?

This is also what we thought, in the list presented to you there is an option to create your own list! And you have the option to choose whether the item you brought will appear to you on each trip or only on this trip. You also have the option to select from the list only what you want, and you will be presented with the narrower list.

At the entrance to the site I answer a personal questionnaire, and what if I want to change my answers?

We also thought about this, you have the option of updating the data where you will be shown the questions and answers you answered and you have the option to change your answer at any given moment, and at the same time, we update your list according to the changes.
